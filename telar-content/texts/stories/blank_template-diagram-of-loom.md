Here is a diagram of a loom and it depicts the parts/tools within a loom. The visual allows us to see how specific tools were used. The loom was the main staple to how textiles where created within the Andean culture. The objects that we looked at are parts of the loom as a whole, with the object we were assigned we can see that they are objects D and E within the diagram. Object D is the batten or sword and object E bobbin and these are important parts of the loom as the bobbin is used to hold and mangage thread while the batten/sword helps push the yarn into place.
