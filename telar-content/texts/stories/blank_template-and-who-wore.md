These garments were worn by people of distinct status to show off their rank which would be shown through a range of simple to elaborately decorated garments with intricate designs or shells, feathers or gold. This was done during religious ceremonies or when given as a religious offering. These garments were also gifted to new leaders, elites, and soldiers, as well as surrounding leaders as a possible way to form an alliance.
