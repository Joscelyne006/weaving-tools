---
title: "Sourcing Materials "
---

The wool would have been taken from camelid animals like llamas, alpacas, and vicunas. The other option would be from plant fibers like cotton or even agave. These were both used to make cloth and finer textiles. These fibers were also dyed to change the thread colors. On other more elite textiles, they would be further decorated with luxury goods like shells, gold, silver, and feathers to add even more of a special touch to the garments. These additions could be used to show the textile was owned by one of the elites of the civilization.
